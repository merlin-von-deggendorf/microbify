About Our Project

We are working on a digitalization project in collaboration with Microbify, a German agricultural company. Our goal is to help farmers prevent fungal growth in crops using AI-based solutions.

Our Mobile App

The mobile app allows farmers to take a picture of a plant. AI analyzes the image to detect fungal infection and recommends the appropriate Microbify treatment.
