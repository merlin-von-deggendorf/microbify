About Us

AgroAi is a smart digital assistant designed to support farmers in identifying plant diseases quickly and easily. Our platform allows users—especially grapevine farmers—to upload photos of their affected plants. In just a few moments, AgroAi analyzes the image using artificial intelligence, detects the disease, and recommends effective treatment solutions, including eco-friendly products from our trusted partner Microbify. The idea behind AgroAi was born at the Digitalisation College in Deggendorf (Campus Cham), where a group of passionate students came together to solve a real-world problem: helping farmers make fast, accurate decisions when dealing with plant illnesses like mildew. Our mission is simple—empower farmers with technology.

We want to make plant disease detection more accessible, reduce crop loss, and support sustainable farming practices. With AgroAi, there’s no need for complex tools or expert knowledge. A single photo is enough to get real, practical advice. We are committed to innovation, simplicity, and positive impact. Our long-term vision is to become a trusted tool for farmers around the world—starting with vineyards and growing beyond. Whether you're managing a large agricultural operation or a small family farm, AgroAi is here to help you grow smarter.
